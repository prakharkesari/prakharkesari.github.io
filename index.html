~ About Me ~

Hi, I’m Prakhar Kesari, an M.Tech student at IIIT Hyderabad specializing in Computer Science and Information Security. My interests span across cybersecurity, cryptography, and systems design. I enjoy tackling complex problems in secure systems, exploring the intricacies of operating systems, and developing tools that improve digital security. With a strong foundation in programming and a passion for innovation, I’m focused on contributing to cutting-edge research and projects that push the boundaries of technology.

Beyond the technical world, I have a deep passion for music and enjoy listening to various genres; sometimes, I even pick up the guitar to play. I am also an avid traveler who loves exploring new places, whether it's solo travelling, trekking through rugged trails, watching serene sunsets at beaches, camping in the mountains, or going on long bike rides to discover hidden gems. These experiences fuel my creativity and give me a fresh perspective on life.
